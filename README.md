### :computer: Work Summary - Mathematics Teacher

I enjoy thinking about and teaching logic, type theory, and their implementation in proof assistants. I use this repository as a place to store my teaching materials.

When I was a grad-student I worked on the arithmetic geometry of semirings: thinking about how the work of Alexander Grothendick could be extended beyond the category of rings to the category of semirings i.e. schemes over the natural numbers. My advisor and I formulated Grothendieck's theory of the fundamental group for schemes over semirings. As a result we were able to calculate the fundamental group of the (spectrum of the) natural numbers - it's the trivial group. My thesis can be found [here](https://maths-people.anu.edu.au/~borger/student-theses/Robert%20Culling%27s%20thesis.pdf).
